# MercyChain Roadmap

## Phase 1: Foundation
- Smart contract architecture (DNAR, Escrow, Cap, Burn)
- Digital DNA system setup
- Landing page and community waitlist

## Phase 2: MVP Launch
- Deploy DNAR and Escrow contracts
- Mobile/web app v1
- 1,000 seed wallet activation
- Test ripple minting and referrals

## Phase 3: Community Growth
- Mainnet launch
- Token sale via escrow model
- DAO proposal + rep elections
- Onboard verified business wallets

## Phase 4: Ecosystem Expansion
- DNA marketplace
- Staking and rewards
- Open developer access

## Phase 5: Full Decentralization
- DAO takes full control
- Seed voter role becomes honorary
- Global MercyChain expansion
